How We Started

Bunny Daycare startedd in Mansfeild, OH by community members looking out for eachother's pets. We recognize that our little fiends don't get the same popularity as dogs or cats might; so not many people around us may know how to take care of them. Our mission it to be your reliable neighbor, here to lend a hand whenever you need. It is hard to leave behind our little loved ones while keeping up with busy day-to-day life. Bunndy Daycare is here to take care of your pets in a safe and well watched enviroment. You can rest easy and enjoy your vacation!
Everyone needs a vacation, including your precious pets! No need to worry about your pet on your next buisness trip or vacation. No matter what your plans are, we are open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Frequentally Asked Questions

  • addWhy not go to a regular kennel?
    We specilize in taking care of small animals. There is a big difference in the needs of pet rabbits and pet dogs. Though we love all furry friends, our facilities keep your pets anxiety free from larger pets.
  • addHow much supervision will there be?
    We have staff at the daycare 24/7! There will always be a trained professional near by to take care of your pets needs such as feeding, cleaning, and bathroom needs. Our veterinary services are from the hours of 8am-8pm every day.
  • addHow are emergencies delt with?
    Our veterinarians are on site from 8am-8pm but there is always someone on call and ready to help. Since we have 24/7 caretakers, emergencies are delt with quickly and we will always pick up your call.
